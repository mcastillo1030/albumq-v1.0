What is AlbumQ?
AlbumQ is an app designed to orgnize your music and your thoughts during music discovery. You'll find the app useful if your main method of discovery is by listening to full records (you know, like they used to do when all they had was vynil?). This app will allow you to add an album you're interested in listening to your queue. From there, you can record your thoughts on each album as you listen, or go back and change your mind on re-listens.

About the developer

This app was developed by Marlon Castillo as a passion project. He loves discovering new music, and has shifted towards listening to full albums at a time in order to gain an impression of the music as a whole. Little by little, he'd record little bits and pieces of information like "I really liked this one song," or, "I liked this lyric because...". As the notes grew, the whole process became cumbersome and inefficient, so he decided as a developer to do something about it. This app is the fruit of that labor.

The app makes use of the Spotify Web API for content loading, and it relies on PHP and AngularJS to make it a simple-to-use Single Page Application (SPA).
